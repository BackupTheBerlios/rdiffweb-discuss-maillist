We want to set up a monitored backup with rdiffweb to all my repos (differents users)

I have two ideas to do it,	
	- over rss (my rss's don't won't to see rdiffweb btw)
	- over sh which search all rdiff-backup-data and do rdiff-backup --verify

Someone have something like that?

My idea to sh is something like this:

find /home -type d -name rdiff-backup-data > CUENTAS
sed 's/rdiff-backup-data//g' CUENTAS >> CUENTAS2
for CUENTA in `cat CUENTAS2`
do
        echo $CUENTA >> CONTROL
        rdiff-backup --verify $CUENTA >> CONTROL
done
