Hello

I try to setup rdiffweb behind this URL:
   https://rdiff.foo.com/

This "almost" work I can navigate my full rdiff-backups but the
links:
- /doLogout
- and the login_form action /doLogin

redirect me on http://localhost:3080/ !






Here is my Apache2/0 VirtualHost

<IfDefine SSL>
   <IfModule mod_ssl.c>
     <VirtualHost *:443>
       ServerName rdiff.foo.com:443
       ServerAdmin luis at localhost
       SSLEngine on
       SSLCertificateFile conf/ssl/server.crt
       SSLCertificateKeyFile conf/ssl/server.key
       <Location />
         AuthType Basic
         AuthName "PrivateArea"
         AuthUserFile  /etc/apache2/auth/foo.com/htaccess
         AuthAuthoritative on
         Require valid-user
       </Location>
       <IfModule mod_rewrite.c>
         RewriteEngine On
         RewriteRule ^/(.*) http://localhost:3080/$1 [P]
       </IfModule>
     </VirtualHost>
   </IfModule>
</IfDefine>


And here my rdiffweb config file.

$ cat /etc/rdiffweb/rdw.conf
ServerPort=3080
UserDB=File
Username=foo
Password=xxx
UserRoot=/
UserRepoPaths=/var/backup/etc|/var/backup/zope29
#UseHttps=True
