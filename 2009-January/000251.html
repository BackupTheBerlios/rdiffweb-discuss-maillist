It looks like we may have a bug that breaks unauthenticated SMTP 
servers. To test this, try removing line 74 (the one that starts with 
"session.login") from email_notification.py. This file can typically be 
found in /usr/lib/python2.5/site-packages/rdiffWeb.

In answer to your question, yes, an email will be sent every day as long 
as the backups are too old.
