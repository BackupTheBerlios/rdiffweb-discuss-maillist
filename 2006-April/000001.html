Couple of comments:
Say you have 6 repositories.  On a given day, you back up 4 of them.  
Two succeed, two fail.  What does the total backup size include?  The 
backups that succeeded today?  All the backups today?  All the 
repositories, period?  It seems a little weird to have an RSS entry 
saying "2 backups suceeded, each with 3GB.  Total size of repositories: 
18GB."

Also, I think it's too expensive to calculate actual disk space usage.  
I suppose we could write a plugin for that that the user could 
optionally enable...

Josh Nisly
