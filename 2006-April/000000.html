If rdiffWeb would include the total backup size in the RSS success 
summary, it would be easier to monitor the size of backups (perhaps for 
bandwidth usage, but especially for remote storage space). I see two 
problems, however:

   1. My backup job includes files on portable storage, which may or may
      not be present. If the portable storage is disconnected, it will
      skew the total backup size.
   2. What number will I see when I run my backup job twice in one day?
      What if my portable storage is available for one, but not the other?

What I really want is the total space occupied by all my repositories, 
but is that possible without thrashing the server?
