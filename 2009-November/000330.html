Hi Chencho,

You could try adding the -d switch to the line in /etc/init.d/rdiff-web 
that starts rdiff-web:

/usr/bin/rdiff-web -d --pid-file="$PIDFILE" \

I always have to have this or rdiff-web does not work at all. But once 
it is started rdiffweb keeps working all the time (except the mail 
feature which seems to be broken).

Dominic
